Yam

Description

Yam (Puna) is very popular in Nigeria and other parts of Africa. You can have boiled yam on your table in under 30 minutes. Yam is a blank canvas, it requires few ingredients to make it and it is very easy to cook too

Ingridients

  • Tuber of Yam
  • salt
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut and peel the tuber of yam
  2. Put water in pot and put on fire
  3. Add peeled yam in the pot
  4. Add salt
  5. Let cook for 30 minutes
  6. Serve with stew or sauce
